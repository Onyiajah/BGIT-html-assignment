Coconut Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 can (about 400 ml) of coconut milk
  • Water
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced red bell pepper and peas to the pan. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. Stir in the rinsed rice and pour in the coconut milk and water. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.
  5. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan with a lid, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed the liquid.
  6. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.
  7. Serve: Serve the coconut rice as a side dish or as a base for your favorite protein.

Cooking Time: 45 minutes
