Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, poached egg

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices to your liking.
  2. Mash the ripe avocado and spread it evenly over the toasted bread.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add your favorite toppings, such as cherry tomatoes or a poached egg.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
